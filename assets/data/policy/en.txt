<div class="description"><b><i>Session Cookies:</i></b> temporary cookies that only remain in your browser until the time you leave the Diginex website, which are used to obtain and store configuration information and administer the Diginex website, including carrying information from one page to another as you browse the site so as to, for example, avoid you having to re-enter information on each page that you visit. <div class="description"><b><i>Persistent Cookies:</i></b> cookies that remain in your browser for a longer period of time for the purpose of compiling anonymous statistics about the use of the Diginex website or to track and record user preferences.
